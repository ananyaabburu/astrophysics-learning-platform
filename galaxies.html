Galaxies

A galaxy is a massive collection of stars, gas, dust, and dark matter held together by gravity.

The Milky Way

Earth is located within the Milky Way galaxy, which contains hundreds of billions of stars.

Types of Galaxies

  • Spiral Galaxies
  • Elliptical Galaxies
  • Irregular Galaxies

Interesting Facts

  • There are billions of galaxies in the observable universe.
  • Galaxies can collide and merge.
  • The Milky Way is a spiral galaxy.
