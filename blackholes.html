Black Holes

A black hole is a region of space where gravity is so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape.

How Do Black Holes Form?

Many black holes form when massive stars run out of fuel and collapse under their own gravity.

What is an Event Horizon?

The event horizon is the boundary surrounding a black hole. Once something crosses this boundary, it cannot escape.

Interesting Facts

  • The first image of a black hole was released in 2019.
  • Black holes can vary greatly in size.
  • Supermassive black holes exist at the centers of many galaxies.
